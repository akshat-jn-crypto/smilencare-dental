About the clinic

Your neighbourhood dental home, led by Dr. Sarika Jain

At Smile'n'Care Dental Clinic in Surya Nagar, Ghaziabad (on the East Delhi border), we treat every patient like family. Led by Dr. Sarika Jain — whose interview has aired on Sahara TV — our focus is gentle, honest, and modern dentistry, using up-to-date techniques and strict hygiene so your visit is safe, comfortable and stress-free.

From a child's first checkup to advanced treatments like implants, root canals and smile design, we offer complete care under one roof. We take time to listen, explain your options clearly, and recommend only what you truly need.

  • Comprehensive treatments for all ages
  • Strict sterilisation & safety protocols
  • Transparent advice & fair pricing
  • Convenient online booking, 7 days a week
