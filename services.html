Our Dental Services

Comprehensive care for the whole family — preventive, restorative, surgical and cosmetic dentistry, all delivered gently and safely.

Prevention & Hygiene

Stopping problems before they start, for every age.

  • Regular dental checkups
  • Dental sealants
  • Fluoride treatment
  • Routine cleaning (scaling & polishing)
  • Oral hygiene education
  • Pediatric (children's) dentistry

Fillings

Repairing decay while keeping teeth looking natural.

  • Tooth-coloured composite fillings
  • Cavity removal & restoration
  • Worn & chipped tooth repair

Periodontal (Gum) Care

Healthy gums are the foundation of healthy teeth.

  • Gingivitis treatment
  • Periodontitis treatment
  • Advanced periodontitis treatment
  • Periodontal intervention & maintenance

Endodontics

Saving natural teeth with pain-free root-canal care.

  • Root canal treatment
  • Endodontic surgery
  • Endodontic diagnosis

Extractions

Comfortable, safe tooth removal when it's truly needed.

  • Normal (simple) extractions
  • Surgical extractions
  • Wisdom tooth removal

Oral Surgery

Minor oral surgical procedures performed with expert care.

  • Minor oral surgical procedures
  • Pre-prosthetic surgery
  • Soft-tissue management

Prosthesis & Implants

Replacing missing teeth to restore your bite and smile.

  • Complete & partial dentures
  • Flexible dentures
  • Zirconia crowns & bridges
  • Dental implants

Smile Design

Reshaping and perfecting your smile, beautifully.

  • Dental veneers
  • Laminates
  • Cosmetic smile makeovers

Teeth Whitening

Safe, professional whitening for a brighter smile.

  • In-clinic professional whitening
  • Stain & discolouration removal
  • Customised whitening advice
