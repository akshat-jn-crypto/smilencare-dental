About the clinic

Caring, modern dentistry you can trust

At Smile'n'Care Dental Clinic, your comfort comes first. Led by Dr. Sarika Jain, we provide gentle, honest and up-to-date dental care for the whole family — from a child's first checkup to advanced treatments like implants, root canals and complete smile makeovers.

We take time to listen, explain your options clearly, and use strict sterilisation so every visit is safe and stress-free.
